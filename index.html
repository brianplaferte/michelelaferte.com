Welcome!

 

I provide licensed psychotherapy services in Providence, Rhode Island for adults living with PTSD, anxiety, panic, and depression. Using mind/body techniques, cognitive interventions, behavioral changes, EMDR therapy and supportive therapy, I can help you make peace with what is – while helping you make changes to improve your daily life.

 

I accept Blue Cross, United, Tufts, and cash payments for psychotherapy services.

 

Please click on the links for more information, or call 401-225-5765 for a free telephone consultation.

 

 

 

The mind and the body are naturally inclined toward health, wellbeing and achievement. EMDR is a powerful tool for quickly identifying and dissolving the memories, ideas, or body sensations that prevent you from doing your best, whatever you’re doing.

 

Whether your goals are in business, the performing arts, or athletics, you know that failure is a part of the risk-taking needed to achieve at high levels. Don’t let past failures bog you down. EMDR will help you process them, learn from them, and let them go.

 

When your body and your mind let go of the past, you will find it easier to live in the moment, returned to your natural state of focus, drive, and confidence.

 

 

Michele Laferte, LMHC
